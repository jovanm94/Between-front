INVOICE FINANCING

Lack of standardization and interoperability between systems companies use, leads to high overhead costs and complex internal processes associated to Open Items Statement matching between counterparties.

SMEs are not using invoice financing products efficiently, offered by financial institutions, since they encounter long and expensive administrative procedures.

The reliance on manual and tedious tasks leads to costly and error-prone processes with potential fraud and compliance issues, unnecessarily lowering counterparty trust in the economy. Focally present when establishing new business relationships between counterparties.

Financial institutions offering invoice financing services base their credit risk assessment on snapshot financial data, missing investment opportunities and increasing risk inherent to the process.

Cash flow fluctuations can lead to significant operational backlogs. Companies in general and especially SMEs encounter difficulties when internally managing short-term liquidity needs

Ecosystem observes fraudulent behaviour associated with double financing.

Platform

The platform in its core integrates invoice verification protocol through company’s upload of issued and received invoices for verification by the counterparty of the transaction, while the event of the transaction is recorded on an immutable distributed ledger. The protocol is powered by Hyperledger Fabric blockchain technology.

Services


Invoice financing

Verified transaction data lays a reliable foundation for financiers to mitigate asymmetric information and offer timely invoice financing that will in turn allow companies to seamlessly overcome cash flow fluctuations and fuel business growth.


Continious liquidity analysis

If you can’t measure it, you can’t manage it. Our platform enables continuous liquidity analysis, a concept previously unattainable, allowing companies to introduce active management system.

About us

Between is a Belgrade-based start-up company founded in 2018. Our team holds deep industry knowledge of the operational problems firms currently face in order to keep their books in check and finance their supply chain, as well as constraints banks encounter during financing approval process. “Between” provides an enterprise-grade platform that ensures the validity of trade transactions between companies and supports invoices financing process, in a single convenient solution. We strive to become an invaluable connection between business entities and banks by leveraging new technologies and offering unique solution to multiple problems present in today’s market.
