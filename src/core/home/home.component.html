About us

Between is a Belgrade based technology company on a mission to enable a smarter and more connected business environment. We are developing a platform that underpins invoice verificiation, financing and continuous liqudity analysis.

We strive to become an invaluable connection between business entities. Providing an enterprise-grade platform that ensures validity of transactions between companies, in a single convenient solution. Resulting in enhancement of counterparty trust, overcoming current lack of standardization and interoperability between companies.

In a Nutshell

CURRENT ISSUES

OUR PROPOSED SOLUTION

COUNTERPARTY RELATIONSHIP

Business entities often encounter counterparty trust issues, leading to costly and timely confirmation process on open item lists. Lack of standardization and interoperability gives rise to disconnected systems.

COUNTERPARTY RELATIONSHIP

Between platform offers invoice verification protocol through registration of invoice information on an immutable distributed ledger through permission based mechanism.

INVOICE FINANCING

Mitigation of cash flow fluctuations through invoice financing is reliant on manual work and followed by complex procedures to reduce risk for financiers, leading to expensive financing solutions. In current state, no direct relationship is established between invoice issuer, invoice financier and invoice payer.

INVOICE FINANCING

Platform users can interact directly through the platform with counterparties and financiers, who can grant invoice financing (direct factoring or/and reverse factoring) based on verified financial data, screening creditworthiness of both parties to the transaction.

FINANCIAL ANALYSIS

The financier is dependent on the information provided by invoice issuer. One of the main obstacles for business entities seeking invoice financing is to prove positive financial history. The same is not possible for a vast majority of small and medium enterprises.

FINANCIAL ANALYSIS

Users have direct access to fully automated financial analytics based on near to real-time data instead of outdated information, through which they can understand and measure business results and targets.

CORE PLATFORM ADVANTAGES

Between platform leverages a core feature of the blockchain called immutability. When an invoice information is stored on the blockchain, it can never be changed. Seamless eco-system with permissioned access and verification protocol brings trust and transparency to all parties involved. Dynamic invoices introduce ease invoice information update reducing paperwork, while preserving sensitive information.



ECOSYSTEM

Invoice verification protocol

Platform integrates invoice verification protocol through registration of invoice information on an immutable distributed ledger that will provide relevant invoice information to the parties of the transaction based on a permissions based mechanism. The protocol is powered by Hyperledger Fabric blockchain technology.

Services


Invoice financing

Verified transaction data lay a reliable foundation for financiers to mitigate asymmetric information and offer timely invoice financing that will in turn allow companies to seamlessly overcome cash flow fluctuations and fuel business growth.


Continious liquidity analysis

If you can’t measure, you can’t manage it. Our platform enables continuous liquidity analysis, a concept previously unattainable. Allowing companies to introduce active management system.
