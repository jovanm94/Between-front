About us

Between is a Belgrade based technology company on a mission to enable a smarter and more connected business environment. We are developing a platform that underpins invoice verificiation, financing and continuous liqudity analysis.

We strive to become an invaluable connection between business entities. Providing an enterprise-grade platform that ensures validity of transactions between companies, in a single convenient solution. Resulting in enhancement of counterparty trust, overcoming current lack of standardization and interoperability between companies.

Invoice verification protocol

Platform integrates invoice verification protocol through registration of invoice information on an immutable distributed ledger that will provide relevant invoice information to the parties of the transaction based on a permissions based mechanism. The protocol is powered by Hyperledged Fabric blockchain technology.

Services


Invoice financing

Verified transaction data lay a reliable foundation for financiers to mitigate asymmetric information and offer timely invoice financing that will in turn allow companies to seamlessly overcome cash flow fluctuations and fuel business growth.


Continious liquidity analysis

If you can’t measure, you can’t manage it. Our platform enables continuous liquidity analysis, a concept previously unattainable. Allowing companies to introduce active management system.
