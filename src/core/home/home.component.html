About us

Between is a Belgrade based technology company on a mission to enable a smarter and more connected business environment. We are developing a platform that underpins invoice verificiation, financing and continuous liqudity analysis.

Invoice verification protocol

Platform integrates invoice verification protocol through registration of invoice information on an immutable distributed ledger that will provide relevant invoice information to the parties of the transaction based on a permissions based mechanism.

Services


Invoice financing

Communicate directly through the platform with counterparties and financiers, receiving funds (direct factoring and/or reverse factoring) based on verified financial data.


Continious liquidity analysis

Direct access to fully automated analytics based on near to real-time financial data.
